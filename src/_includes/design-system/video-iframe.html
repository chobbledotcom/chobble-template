{%- comment -%} Shared video iframe partial with privacy-protecting defaults. Supports both YouTube video IDs and custom iframe URLs (starting with "http"). Custom URLs are used directly as iframe src, while YouTube IDs are converted to privacy-respecting youtube-nocookie.com embed URLs. Parameters: - video_id: YouTube video ID or custom iframe URL (required) - title: Accessible title for the iframe (required) - background: If true, adds mute/loop/controls params for background video (default: false) Both video-background and video-cards use this partial to ensure consistent iframe attributes and privacy settings. {%- endcomment -%}
